Information and Data

People often treat the words "data" and "information" as interchangeable; they are not. you get information by combining, comparing, analyzing, or doing calculations on data. In a way, information is the output of an algorithms.

  • Data: unprocessed material
  • Information: knowledge, intelligence, or a specific piece of data with a unqie significance or purpose.

Database

A database is like a fancy computer filing system: organizing and storing data. A spreadsheet is bascially a database that arrange things in rows and columns for future reference. In computing a database is a structured and centralized repository of data stored on a computer. This system permits the retrival, addition, modification, and deletion of data, along with the capability to conver the retrieved data into meaningful information. A DBA manages databases, which store data in it's orginal form. Querying or retrieiving this data results in its conversion into more valuable information.
